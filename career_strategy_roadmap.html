Your actual moat
9 years Binance P2P Merchant + data analytics
No one applying for P2P analytics roles at Binance has 9 years of verified merchant experience on Binance. You have operated the exact systems you are now analyzing. That is not a credential — that is domain authority.
  • Put "9 years Binance P2P Verified Merchant" in LinkedIn About section

The positioning that wins
Not a generalist. A specialist in one niche.
Generic data analysts compete on price. Specialists compete on value. You are building the only 5-project portfolio in the world focused on African crypto exchange analytics. That makes comparison shopping impossible for a hiring manager who needs that exact expertise.
  • Every post, every project, every application — Africa crypto analytics
  • Never apply for general BI analyst roles — stay in your lane
  • Your LinkedIn tagline is your positioning: say the niche every time
  • Reject irrelevant opportunities — diluting your niche dilutes your brand

Reality check — what accelerates this and what kills it
What gets you to $3K/mo by Month 12
Ship all 5 projects on schedule. Post weekly without missing. Apply from Month 2. Freelance 2–3 small gigs per month. DM 3 people per week. Negotiate your first offer — do not accept first number.
What delays you to Month 18+
Skipping post days. Waiting to apply until "portfolio is perfect". Taking generic BI jobs that dilute your niche. Not freelancing during the gap. Accepting the first salary offered without negotiating.
Your biggest differentiator vs other applicants
You have 9 years of operational P2P experience on Binance. You understand M-Pesa corridors, KES–USDT spreads, merchant behavior from the inside. No data analytics bootcamp produces this. It cannot be taught. It is yours alone.
The one thing that will cost you the most
Inconsistency. Missing a week of the project. Stopping posts after 2 weeks. This career transition compounds exactly like interest — the gains are slow at first and then sudden. The analysts who stop at Month 3 never see the Month 6 inflection.
